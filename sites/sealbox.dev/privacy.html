Privacy Notice

Last updated 27 June 2026

SealBox is a forthcoming developer tool published by Audit&Fix. This notice covers the sealbox.dev website and its waitlist. It does not yet cover the SealBox product itself, which is not released.

What we collect

  • Waitlist email. If you submit the waitlist form, we store the email address you enter.
  • Minimal request metadata sent with that submission: a timestamp, the referring page, your browser user-agent, approximate country, and IP address. This is used only to prevent abuse and understand signup volume.

The site serves static pages over Cloudflare. We don't use advertising trackers or third-party analytics cookies.

Why & how long

We use your email for one purpose: to notify you when SealBox opens, and occasional related updates. Waitlist records are kept until launch and our follow-up, then deleted. You can ask us to remove you at any time and we will.

Where it's stored

Waitlist entries are stored in Cloudflare Workers KV (Cloudflare, Inc.), processing data on our behalf. We do not sell or rent your information, and we don't share it except as needed to operate the waitlist or where required by law.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from any email we send, or email us to be removed.
  • Request access to, or deletion of, the data we hold about you.

Contact

Questions or removal requests: privacy@auditandfix.com.
